About Us

Marine Command Squad: Discipline is our identity

"Deep resolve like the sea, fearless courage like the commando"

Who We Are

Marine Command Squad is a non-profit and non-political voluntary organization (NGO), working to develop the youth of the country in the image of the defense force. Our main goal is to create a group of physically and mentally strong 'Marine Force' through disciplined training, who will play a leading role in serving the country and dealing with disasters.

Inspired by the glorious ideals of the Indian Navy and other military forces, we instill in each of our cadets the mantra of hard work, dedication, and patriotism.

Our Mission & Vision

  • Physical and Mental Excellence: Enhance cadets’ physical capabilities and mental strength to survive adverse situations.
  • Defence Preparation: Provide preliminary guidance and direction to join armed forces including the Indian Navy.
  • Leadership: Develop leadership qualities through teamwork.
  • Social Security: Volunteer alongside administration during national disasters or emergencies.

Why Marine Command Squad?

Our training module aligns with stringent defence recruitment criteria. We believe, "Sweating in peacetime makes preparation for bloody war easier." Every applicant is treated not just as a cadet, but as a future protector. Transparency and military discipline are maintained at every stage, from registration to training.
